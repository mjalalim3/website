--- layout: home author_profile: true --- # About me Hello! I'm Mahmoud Jalali Mehrabad, a researcher at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), working with Prof. Dirk Englund in the [Quantum Photonics & AI Group](https://qp.mit.edu/team). I am passionate about advancing quantum technologies through innovative research in semiconductor and optical systems. My research spans a wide array of topics, from quantum photonics and optical computing to quantum sensing and semiconductor integration. I enjoy collaborating with scientists from diverse fields, bringing together theoretical, computational, and experimental approaches to tackle complex problems in quantum technologies and photonics.
